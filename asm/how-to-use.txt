TO DISASSEMBLE:
asm -d infile outfile [-b 0xbaseaddress] [-r outsymbols] [-l [insymbols]]

ex:
asm -d code.bin code.asm -b 0x80012030 -r syms2.bin -l syms.bin

TO ASSEMBLE:
asm -a infile outfile [-b 0xbaseaddress] [-r outsymbols] [-l [insymbols]]

ex:
asm -a code.asm out.bin -b 0x80012030 -r syms2.bin -l syms.bin
